
Intro to Robotics with XRP
==========================

Getting Started
---------------

* Please open in a new tab: `XRP Code Editor <https://xrpcode.wpi.edu/>`_.
* Pugin the USB cable to your robot and your laptop.
* Click on the "CONNECT XRP" button to sync with your robot.
